Orderoo Privacy Policy:

We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing your data in accordance with applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect from you, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. What data does Orderoo process about me?

Orderoo processes various types of data about you, including:

* Personal data: name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment method, and any other information you provide to us.
* Location data: we collect your geolocation data when you use our app or website to locate restaurants near you.
* User behavior data: we track your interactions with our platform, such as the restaurants you view, order from, or mark as favorites.
* Cookie data: we use cookies and similar technologies to collect data about your device, browser, and usage patterns.
* Social media data: if you connect your social media account to Orderoo, we collect your profile information, friend list, and any other data you choose to share with us.
2. For what purposes does Orderoo use my email address?

We use your email address for the following purposes:

* To send you confirmations, updates, and receipts related to your orders.
* To respond to your inquiries, feedback, and support requests.
* To provide you with news, promotions, and discounts from Orderoo and our partners.
* To personalize your experience and improve our services.
3. Who does Orderoo share my geolocation with?

We share your geolocation data with:

* Restaurants: to provide them with your location for delivery or pickup purposes.
* Service providers: we may share your geolocation data with third-party service providers that help us improve our services, such as mapping and navigation technology providers.
4. What types of data are processed on the basis of consent, and for what purposes?

We process the following data based on your consent:

* Location data: to provide you with location-based services, such as locating restaurants near you.
* User behavior data: to personalize your experience, improve our services, and show you relevant advertising.
* Social media data: to connect your social media account to Orderoo and provide you with personalized content and promotions.
5. What data does Orderoo share with Facebook?

We share the following data with Facebook:

* User behavior data: to track the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns on Facebook.
* Social media data: if you connect your social media account to Orderoo, we share your profile information and friend list with Facebook.
6. Does Orderoo share my data with insurers?

We do not share your data with insurers.
7. What categories of data does Orderoo collect about me automatically?

We collect the following data about you automatically:

* Device data: device type, operating system, browser type, and device language.
* Usage data: pages viewed, interactions with the platform, and other usage patterns.
* Location data: your geolocation data when you use our app or website.

We keep your delivery address for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You can update or remove your delivery address at any time through your account settings.
