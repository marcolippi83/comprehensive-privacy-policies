Q2: For what purposes does Orderoo use my email address?

Orderoo uses your email address for the following purposes:

1. As a unique identifier for setting up and logging into your account
2. To allow you to reset your password if forgotten
3. To send you receipts for your orders
4. To distribute advertisements of Orderoo's own services
5. To send you third-party marketing advertisements 
6. To track transaction history for investigative authorities if needed
7. To use the domain name part of your email for profiling.
